           visitor_mary
           curator_sarah
           ```

           - `gallery_name`
           - `floor`
           - `capacity`
           - `theme`
           - `status`

           3|Science Discovery Center|3|40|Science|Renovation

           - `gallery_id`
           - `exhibition_type`
           - `start_date`
           - `end_date`

         ### 4. `artifacts.txt`

           - `description`
